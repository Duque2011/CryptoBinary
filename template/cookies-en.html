COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: March 7, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how CRYPTO BINARY LTD. ("Company," "we," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website https://www.cryptobinary.top ("Site"). Here, we describe what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

In some cases, cookies may collect personal information or information that becomes personal when combined with other data.

WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small data files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to ensure the proper functioning of websites, improve efficiency, and generate reports.

First-party cookies: Set by us (Crypto Binary).

Third-party cookies: Set by partners (e.g., data analytics providers).

WHY DO WE USE COOKIES?

Essential Cookies: Necessary to operate the Site (e.g., access to secure areas, authentication).

Performance and Analytics: Monitor traffic and optimize user experience.

Targeted Advertising: Personalize ads based on your browsing behavior.

Security and Compliance: Detect suspicious activities and ensure regulatory compliance.

HOW TO CONTROL COOKIES

Consent Manager: Access the cookie banner or the settings section of the Site to accept/reject specific categories (except essential cookies).

Browser Settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy

Opt-out of Targeted Advertising:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (USA)
  • European Alliance

OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Web Beacons (Pixels): We use invisible pixels in emails and pages to monitor engagement (e.g., clicks on promotional campaigns for binary options).

Local Storage (LocalStorage): Stores trading session data to ensure security and continuity.

FLASH COOKIES (LSOs)

We may use Local Shared Objects (Flash Cookies) to:

  • Prevent fraud in transactions;
  • Store interface preferences;
  • Ensure performance in real-time trading charts.

To manage LSOs:

  • Access Adobe Storage Settings.
  • Adjust permissions or delete existing data.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

MiFID II/ESMA (EU): Cookies related to risk warnings and consent are stored to comply with legal obligations.

LGPD/GDPR: We record your cookie preferences to ensure transparency and compliance with data protection laws.
